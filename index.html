Musicians

This year we have invited top musician representing different styles of jazz music. We have diversity of styles: from traditional swing, bebop musician, through ethnic influences on jazz music to contemporary, experimental electronic music. Each day fans of jazz may find something interestin, according to personal preferences.

Scenes

Our artists will give performances on three stages. The main stage is situated in the theter in the city center. There is also alternative scene located 500 meters away. The third scene is experimental, with different musicians and different styles.

Other events

There are aditional events in the city like films projections - all about jazz music, discussions and meetings with musicians. There are clubs where you can dance to jazz music!!!
