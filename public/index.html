Program

Musicians

This year we have invited top musician representing different styles of jazz music. We have diversity of styles: from traditional swing, bebop musician, through ethnic influences on jazz music to contemporary, experimental electronic music. Each day fans of jazz may find something interesting, according to personal preferences.

Scenes

Our artists will give performances on three stages. The main stage is situated in the theater in the city center. There is also alternative scene located 500 meters away. The third scene is experimental, with different musicians and different styles.

Other events

There are additional events in the city like films projections - all about jazz music, discussions and meetings with musicians. There are clubs where you can dance to jazz music!!!

Quizzes

Try our quizzes about jazz music, jazz history or general music knowledge.

Archive festivals

Our Contemporary Jazz Festival has long tradition. The first edition took place in 2018. Check our previous editions and artists performing in every year.
